Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SpinMaster Academy.

What equipment do I need to bring?

We provide professional paddles and practice balls for beginners during training sessions. We recommend bringing personal athletic wear and non-marking indoor court shoes. Advanced players are encouraged to bring their custom paddles.

Can I pause or modify my membership?

Yes, you can pause or upgrade/downgrade your active plan at any time. Simply notify our front desk at least 5 business days before your next billing cycle.

How do I book 1-on-1 private coaching sessions?

Our Pro membership includes one private session per month. You can book additional slots with your preferred certified coach online, via the front desk, or by calling our support line.

Are there programs for junior players?

Yes! We have an intensive Junior Development Program for young athletes aged 6 to 16, which covers motor agility, tactical placement, and tournament preparation.
